    My name is Artöm Ognöv, I'm comic book artist, illustrator and game developer. This site is something like my portfolio.

    I'm open for paid collaborations and freelance projects. Anyway you can contact me via e-mail or Telegram. Let's make some great stuff!

    Comics

    When I was 6 I was bitten by a radioactive comic book artist. Since then I can't stop drawing. I think comics are the most expressive medium in human history.
